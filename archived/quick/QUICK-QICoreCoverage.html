QICoreCoverage

Financial instrument which may be used to reimburse or pay for health care products and services. Includes both insurance and self-payment.

= Must Support, = Is Modifier, = QiCore defined extension

FieldCard.TypeDescription
identifier0..*List<Identifier>A unique identifier assigned to this coverage.
status1..1StringThe status of the resource instance.
Binding: A code specifying the state of the resource instance. (required)
type0..1ConceptThe type of coverage: social program, medical plan, accident coverage (workers compensation, auto), group health or payment by an individual or organization.
Binding: Categories of types of health care payor entities as defined by the US Public Health Data Consortium SOP code system (required)
policyHolder0..1QICorePatient | QICoreOrganization | QICoreRelatedPersonThe party who 'owns' the insurance policy.
subscriber0..1QICorePatient | QICoreRelatedPersonThe party who has signed-up for or 'owns' the contractual relationship to the policy or to whom the benefit of the policy for services rendered to them or their family is due.
subscriberId0..1StringThe insurer assigned ID for the Subscriber.
beneficiary1..1QICorePatientThe party who benefits from the insurance coverage; the patient when products and/or services are provided.
dependent0..1StringA unique identifier for a dependent under the coverage.
relationship0..1ConceptThe relationship of beneficiary (patient) to the subscriber.
Binding: The relationship between the Subscriber and the Beneficiary (insured/covered party/patient). (extensible)
period0..1Interval<DateTime>Time period during which the coverage is in force. A missing start date indicates the start date isn't known, a missing end date means the coverage is continuing to be in force.
payor1..*List<QICorePatient | QICoreOrganization | QICoreRelatedPerson>The program or plan underwriter or payor including both insurance and non-insurance agreements, such as patient-pay agreements.
class0..*List<class>A suite of underwriter specific classifiers.
order0..1IntegerThe order of applicability of this coverage relative to other coverages which are currently in force. Note, there may be gaps in the numbering and this does not imply primary, secondary etc. as the specific positioning of coverages depends upon the episode of care.
network0..1StringThe insurer-specific identifier for the insurer-defined network of providers to which the beneficiary may seek treatment which will be covered at the 'in-network' rate, otherwise 'out of network' terms and conditions apply.
costToBeneficiary0..*List<costToBeneficiary>A suite of codes indicating the cost category and associated amount which have been detailed in the policy and may have been included on the health card.
subrogation0..1BooleanWhen 'subrogation=true' this insurance instance has been included not for adjudication but to provide insurers with the details to recover costs.
contract0..*List<Contract>The policy(s) which constitute this insurance coverage.
