QICoreDeviceUseStatement

A record of a device being used by a patient where the record is the result of a report from the patient or another clinician.

= Must Support, = Is Modifier, = QiCore defined extension

FieldCard.TypeDescription
identifier0..*List<Identifier>An external identifier for this statement such as an IRI.

Binding: A coded concept indicating the current status of the Device Usage. (required)
