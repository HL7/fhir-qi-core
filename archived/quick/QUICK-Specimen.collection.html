Specimen.collection

Details concerning the specimen collection.

= Must Support, = Is Modifier, = QiCore defined extension

FieldCard.TypeDescription
collector0..1QICorePractitionerPerson who collected the specimen.
collected[x]0..1DateTime | Interval<DateTime>Time when specimen was collected from subject - the physiologically relevant time.
duration0..1DurationThe span of time over which the collection of a specimen occurred.
quantity0..1QuantityThe quantity of specimen collected; for instance the volume of a blood sample, or the physical measurement of an anatomic pathology sample.
method0..1ConceptA coded value specifying the technique that is used to perform the procedure.
Binding: The technique that is used to perform the procedure. (example)
bodySite0..1ConceptAnatomical location from which the specimen was collected (if subject is a patient). This is the target site. This element is not used for environmental specimens.
Binding: Codes describing anatomical locations. May include laterality. (example)
fastingStatus[x]0..1Concept | DurationAbstinence or reduction from some or all food, drink, or both, for a period of time prior to sample collection.
Binding: Codes describing the fasting status of the patient. (extensible)
