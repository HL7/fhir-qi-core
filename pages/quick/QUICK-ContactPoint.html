ContactPoint

Details for all kinds of technology mediated contact points for a person or organization, including telephone, email, etc.

= Must Support, = Is Modifier, = QiCore defined extension

FieldCard.TypeDescription
system0..1StringTelecommunications form for contact point - what communications system is required to make use of the contact.
value0..1StringThe actual contact point details, in a form that is meaningful to the designated communication system (i.e. phone number or email address).
use0..1StringIdentifies the purpose for the contact point.
rank0..1IntegerSpecifies a preferred order in which to use a set of contacts. Contacts are ranked with lower values coming before higher values.
period0..1Interval<DateTime>Time period when the contact point was/is in use.
