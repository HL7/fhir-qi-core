HumanName

A human's name with the ability to identify parts and usage.

= Must Support, = Is Modifier, = QiCore defined extension

FieldCard.TypeDescription
use0..1StringIdentifies the purpose for this name.
text0..1StringA full text representation of the name.
family0..1StringThe part of a name that links to the genealogy. In some cultures (e.g. Eritrea) the family name of a son is the first name of his father.
given0..*StringGiven name.
prefix0..*StringPart of the name that is acquired as a title due to academic, legal, employment or nobility status, etc. and that appears at the start of the name.
suffix0..*StringPart of the name that is acquired as a title due to academic, legal, employment or nobility status, etc. and that appears at the end of the name.
period0..1Interval<DateTime>Indicates the period of time when this name was valid for the named person.
