CarePlan.activity

Identifies a planned action to occur as part of the plan. For example, a medication to be used, lab tests to perform, self-monitoring, education, etc.

= Must Support, = Is Modifier, = QiCore defined extension

FieldCard.TypeDescription
outcomeCodeableConcept0..*List<Concept>Identifies the outcome at the point when the status of the activity is assessed. For example, the outcome of an education activity could be patient understands (or not).
Binding: Identifies the results of the activity. (example)
outcomeReference0..*List<Resource>Details of the outcome or action resulting from the activity. The reference to an "event" resource, such as Procedure or Encounter or Observation, is the result/outcome of the activity itself. The activity can be conveyed using CarePlan.activity.detail OR using the CarePlan.activity.reference (a reference to a “request” resource).
progress0..*List<Annotation>Notes about the adherence/status/progress of the activity.
reference0..1Appointment | QICoreCommunicationRequest | QICoreDeviceNotRequested | QICoreMedicationNotRequested | QICoreNutritionOrder | QICoreTask | QICoreServiceNotRequested | VisionPrescription | RequestGroupThe details of the proposed activity represented in a specific resource.
detail0..1activity.detailA simple summary of a planned activity suitable for a general care plan system (e.g. form driven) that doesn't know about specific resources such as procedure etc.
