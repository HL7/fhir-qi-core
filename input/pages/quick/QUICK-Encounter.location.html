Encounter.location

List of locations where the patient has been during this encounter.

= Must Support, = Is Modifier, = QiCore defined extension

FieldCard.TypeDescription
location1..1QICoreLocationThe location where the encounter takes place.
status0..1StringThe status of the participants' presence at the specified location during the period specified. If the participant is no longer at the location, then the period will have an end date/time.
Binding: The status of the location. (required)
physicalType0..1ConceptThis will be used to specify the required levels (bed/ward/room/etc.) desired to be recorded to simplify either messaging or query.
Binding: Physical form of the location. (example)
period0..1Interval<DateTime>Time period during which the patient was present at the location.
